Shree Balloons — Born from Passion

Shree Balloons was born from a simple belief — every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. Founded in 2019 in Delhi, we started with birthday decorations and a dream to bring smiles to every doorstep.


Today, we've decorated 5000+ events across 7+ cities in Delhi NCR, from intimate room surprise decorations to grand wedding stages. Our team of dedicated artists puts love into every setup they create.

📅 Our Journey

How We Got Here

🌱

2019 — Founded

Started with birthday decorations in Delhi. Got our first 50 clients in the very first month through word of mouth.

🚀

2020 — Growth Despite Odds

Pivoted to outdoor and socially-distanced setups. Expanded our design catalogue and built a loyal customer base.

📈

2021 — Expanded Services

Added weddings, ring ceremonies, haldi, mehendi & baby showers. Crossed 1000+ events decorated.

🌟

2022 — Multi-City Expansion

Expanded services to Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Ghaziabad and more. Built a dedicated team of 15+ artists.

🏆

2024 — 5000+ Events

Became Delhi NCR's most-reviewed balloon decoration brand on Google with a 4.9★ average rating.
